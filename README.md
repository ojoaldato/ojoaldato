### Principles

- 1 is better than 2, and 2 is better than 0. Don't couple teams early. Optimize for throughput.
- Problems first, tools second. Start with the pain point, not the shiny tech stack.
- Ship fast on solid foundations. Use SOLID principles so fast doesn't become broken.
- Keep it simple. Complexity is a tax you will have to pay forever.
- UX over tech ego. The user’s experience always beats the developer's pride.
- Data is leverage. Your code is the engine, but the data is your real advantage.
- AI is bigger than GenAI. Stick to first principles and stay skeptical of experts and hype.
- Skip the premature optimization. It really is the root of all evil.
